Description

Instant noodles are a Japanese noodle dish, sold in a dried and precooked noodle block, with flavoring powder and/or seasoning oil. The main ingredients used in dried noodles are usually wheat flour, palm oil, and salt. Common ingredients in the flavoring powder are salt, monosodium glutamate, seasoning, and sugar.

Ingredients

  • Ramen
  • Hot water
  • Flavor packet

Directions

  1. Put Ramen in bowl
  2. Add water to bowl
  3. Microwave for two minutes
  4. Add flavor packet and stir
  5. Eat and enjoy
